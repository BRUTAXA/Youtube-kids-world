# ART — Visual Research

ART is the visual research layer of AI Kids Studio.

It stores experiments, visual exploration and component studies.

---

## Principle

ART is not a prompt catalog.

Prompts are temporary tools. The durable asset is the selected visual decision and the reason why it won.

---

## Phase 2 Start

Current priority:

```text
ART-001 — Visual DNA Research
├── ART-001.001 — Eye Research
├── ART-001.002 — Head Research
├── ART-001.003 — Hair Research
├── ART-001.004 — Face Research
└── ART-001.005 — Body Research
```

After these studies, the project will assemble the first complete Max and move to storyboard and animatic.

---

## Experiment naming

Use experiments instead of “variants”.

Examples:

```text
Experiment E001 — Large round eyes
Experiment E002 — Soft almond eyes
Experiment E003 — Gentle dot eyes
```

Each experiment can be:

- Candidate
- Approved pending convergence pass
- Final Approved
- Confirmed by OBS
- Overturned by OBS

---

## Convergence pass

Individual components are not final until seen together on a complete character.

Eyes, head, hair, face and body influence each other.

The first complete Max will trigger a convergence pass before final visual decisions are locked.
