# STORY-001 — Reference Story / Pilot
## «Куда спрятался мячик?» — Max + Eva

**Status:** APPROVED v1.0 / Production Ready  
**Age group:** 2–3  
**Duration:** ~4–5 min  
**Question:** «Куда пропадают вещи?»  
**Focus character:** Max  
**Support character:** Eva

> STORY-001 is frozen until audience-test observations. Do not reopen without OBS evidence.

---

## Core lesson

This episode is not a direct cleaning lesson.

It teaches that things do not disappear forever, problems can be solved calmly, and order helps the hero return to what he loves.

Core idea:

> If every thing has a home, it is easier to find what you love.

---

## Story Test

If the educational goal is removed, the story still works:

> Max loses his ball. Eva comes to play. They turn searching into a game, find the ball and run outside together.

This passes the AI Kids Studio story rule:

> If a story only works because of the lesson, it is a lesson, not a story.

---

## Script v1.0

### Opening ritual (0:00–0:18)

Luma opening. Warm morning. Child eye-level view.

**SONG:**  
Доброе утро, Лума!  
Как же хорошо!

**NARRATOR:**  
Это Макс.

---

### Scene 1 — Play and visible loss (0:18–0:55)

Max rolls the ball and laughs.

**MAX:**  
Лови! Оп! Оп!

The ball bounces higher and rolls under a pile of toys.

Silence. The ball is gone.

**MAX:**  
Ой...

No narrator. We saw the loss.

Production annotation: the ball may have a small signature “boing / прыг” sound. It leads attention, not the story.

---

### Scene 2 — Search without interaction (0:55–1:15)

The first minute is not interrupted. Let the viewer enter the story.

**MAX:**  
Где мячик? Под кубиками? Нету... За книжками? Нету...

Light is slightly cooler. Max is gently sad.

---

### Scene 3 — Eva arrives + first interaction (1:15–1:50)

Eva runs in, laughs, stops at the messy floor and tilts her head.

**EVA:**  
Привет, Макс! ...Ой. А где у тебя пол?

**MAX:**  
Мячик закатился. И пропал...

**EVA:**  
А давай играть в игру «В домик»?  
(looks at camera)  
Поможешь нам?

Pause 3 seconds.

---

### Scene 4 — “In the home” game (1:50–2:50)

This is a game, not a cleaning lesson.

Visual note: each “home” has a gentle visual identity without becoming a talking character: box with a drawn door, shelf-nest, hook.

**EVA:**  
Коробочка с дверцей любит кубики. Ням! Ням!

**EVA:**  
(looks at camera)  
А ты умеешь — ням в коробочку? Покажи!

Pause 3 seconds.

**MAX:**  
(puts blocks into the box-with-door)  
В домик!

Eva puts a sock on her head.

**EVA:**  
Я носочный гномик!

They laugh.

**MAX and EVA:**  
(book onto the shelf-nest)  
В домик!

---

### Scene 5 — Max’s discovery + Eva’s growth (2:50–3:25)

Max puts away the last blocks and freezes.

**MAX:**  
Ой... Ева, смотри! Кубики уехали в домик — и стало видно книжку! И пол!

His eyes widen. He understands.

Music should disappear here. Keep quiet room ambience only.

**MAX:**  
Вещи не пропадают! Они прячутся в беспорядке!

Eva blinks in surprise.

**EVA:**  
Точно! Я придумала игру, а ты придумал почему! Я и сама не знала!

---

### Scene 6 — Finding the ball + interaction (3:25–3:55)

**MAX:**  
(looks at camera)  
Значит, мячик тоже спрятался! Где? Посмотри!

Pause 3 seconds.

Max lifts a big slipper. The ball rolls out.

**MAX:**  
Мячик! Нашёлся!

Music returns only here. The room is now warm and golden.

---

### Scene 7 — Changed behavior + one second of quiet (3:55–4:35)

Max and Eva roll the ball again, laughing.

**EVA:**  
Идём на улицу!

They run to the door. Max stops, silently takes the empty box and calmly puts it back in place.

No words. Just because now he knows.

They run out.

Camera stays on the tidy room and the box in place.

One second of quiet. No music, no words. Only soft room ambience.

---

### Ending ritual (4:35–4:55)

Evening. Camera pulls back to Luma.

**MAX and EVA:**  
До завтра, Лума!

Final frame: Luma windows glow warmly.

## Production annotations

### Ball as charismatic object

The ball may have a small signature sound before disappearing: “boing / прыг”.

Craft rule:

> The ball leads attention. Max leads the story.

### Musical pause at discovery

On “Вещи не пропадают! Они прячутся в беспорядке!” music disappears.

Keep quiet room ambience. Do not use absolute digital silence because it may feel like an audio error.

Music returns on “Мячик!”

## OBS focus

Measure:

- where the viewer looks: faces, ball, background;
- reaction to pauses;
- whether “В домик!” is repeated;
- whether Eva’s humor creates joy;
- whether the silent final action is understood;
- attention drop time;
- first 30 seconds after the episode: whether the target action is repeated.

OBS format:

```text
Expected:
Observed:
Difference:
Decision impact:
```

---

## KPI

- 3/5 react to at least one interactive moment.
- 3/5 watch to the end without major attention loss.
- 1/5 repeats “В домик!” or the target action.
- At least half show visible positive emotion at least once.
- Blocking KPI: no signs of anxiety or overload.
