# Max — Foundation v1

**Status:** APPROVED / Foundation v1  
**Character:** Max  
**Age group:** 2–3  
**Current review state:** Internal Art Review approved  
**Children Review / OBS:** Pending

---

## Core Emotion

> Helping feels natural.

Max does not help to be praised.  
Max does not help to teach.  
Max helps because he cares.

---

## Child Feeling

> I feel safe with him.

A child should feel that Max is a friend who is there when you need him.

---

## Natural Action

Max picks something up before anyone asks.

His care is expressed through action, posture and timing, not through explanation.

---

## Personality

Max is:

- kind;
- reliable;
- warm;
- slightly forgetful because he cares about others;
- ready to help his friends naturally.

---

## Visual Foundation

Max Foundation v1 is based on:

- Family A from ART-001 Character Test;
- a small spark from Family B, without changing Max into a show-off;
- caring body language;
- warm hybrid painted material;
- soft, rounded forms;
- small warm imperfections;
- a slightly messy, never-perfect silhouette.

---

## Never

Max must not become:

- a show-off;
- a teacher;
- a perfect “good kid”;
- a moral instruction device.

---

## Production Rule

Do not redesign Max from scratch.

Iterate only from this foundation unless Children Test / OBS proves that the design does not work for children.
