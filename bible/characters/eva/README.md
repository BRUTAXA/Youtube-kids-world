# Eva

**Status:** Approved (Art Review)  
**Foundation:** v1  
**Children Review:** Pending

Eva is one of the six core children of AI Kids Studio.

She represents curiosity, adventure, movement and joyful exploration.

Eva is designed to gently pull Max outside his comfort zone, while Max naturally brings calm and safety back to Eva.

---

## Core Emotion

**Adventure feels exciting.**

Eva doesn't look for danger.

She discovers wonder.

---

## Relationship

**Max ↔ Eva**

Eva gives Max:

- Adventure
- Energy
- Curiosity

Max gives Eva:

- Calm
- Safety
- Confidence

Together they create the emotional balance of the series.

---

## Visual Language

Inherited from the AI Kids Studio visual language:

- Hybrid Material
- Warm palette
- Rounded shapes
- Warm imperfections
- Child-first readability
