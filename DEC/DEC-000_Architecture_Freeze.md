# DEC-000 — Architecture Freeze

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Type:** Design / Process Decision  
**Phase:** End of Phase 1

---

## Decision

The architecture of AI Kids Studio is considered complete enough to start production.

New architecture layers, new document families and new process structures are frozen unless real observations prove that continuing without them is impossible.

> **Reality over Architecture.**

---

## Reason

The project now has enough foundation to move forward:

- AKS — foundation and philosophy
- ART — visual research
- DEC — design decisions
- OBS — real-world observations
- STORY — production scripts
- GitHub — source of truth
- Children Test — reality check

Adding more architecture now would delay the first real contact with children.

The project must now learn from images, animatics and real reactions.

---

## Allowed after freeze

The following are allowed:

- ART experiments
- STORY production work
- Storyboards
- Animatics
- Children tests
- OBS records
- DEC updates based on evidence
- Minor updates to existing documents when production proves the need

---

## Not allowed after freeze

The following are not allowed without strong OBS evidence:

- new architecture layers;
- new document families;
- new large methodology documents;
- theoretical rewrites that do not move the project closer to children seeing a frame.

---

## Gatekeeper question

Before adding anything new, ask:

> Does this bring us closer to the first child watching the first episode?

If the answer is no, do not add it.

---

## Notes

OBS can reopen decisions, but the bar must be high.

A new layer is allowed only when the project cannot continue without it — not because it would be elegant or interesting.
