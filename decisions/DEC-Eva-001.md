# DEC-Eva-001 — Eva Foundation v1

**Status:** Approved  
**Date:** 2026-06-26  
**Area:** Character Design / ART-002  
**Character:** Eva  
**Children Review / OBS:** Pending

---

## Decision

Approve **Eva Foundation v1** as the official second core character of AI Kids Studio.

Eva becomes part of the permanent Character Library.

Children Review remains pending.

---

## Core Emotion

> Adventure feels exciting.

Eva is the child who naturally invites others into discovery.

She doesn't seek attention.

She shares excitement.

---

## Role

Eva gently pulls Max toward adventure.

Max gently brings Eva back to calm.

Their relationship forms one of the emotional foundations of the studio.

---

## Visual Language

Inherited from AI Kids Studio visual language:

- Hybrid Material
- Warm Palette
- Rounded Shapes
- Warm Imperfections
- Child-first readability

---

## Approved Foundation

Finalist A from ART-002 Eva Character Emotion Research.

---

## Why This Was Approved

Eva Foundation v1 is approved because:

- relationship with Max is immediately readable;
- silhouette is clearly distinguishable from Max;
- energy complements Max instead of duplicating him;
- visual language remains consistent with the studio;
- her body language reads as “Come on, it will be fun” before dialogue;
- she expands the world from one character into a believable friendship pair.

---

## What Must Be Preserved

Future iterations must preserve:

- forward movement;
- joyful invitation;
- clear silhouette with twin ponytails;
- warm child-safe energy;
- contrast with Max;
- same world language as Max;
- playful imperfection.

---

## What Must Be Avoided

Do not turn Eva into:

- a show-off;
- a reckless child;
- a loud hyperactive character;
- a clone of Max;
- a character who ignores friends;
- a design that relies only on accessories instead of personality.

---

## Known Open Risks

These are accepted risks until Children Review:

1. Eyes may still be slightly too large or glossy.
2. Final render may drift toward high-detail CGI.
3. Eva may become too energetic if not balanced by Max.
4. Children may prefer a calmer Eva than adults expect.

These risks must be validated by Children Test / OBS, not by endless adult iteration.

---

## Children Review Rule

Eva Foundation v1 may be changed after:

- Children Test shows weak emotional connection;
- children do not read her as inviting or joyful;
- children are overstimulated by her energy;
- OBS shows anxiety, confusion or lack of interest.

Until then, do not redesign Eva from scratch.
